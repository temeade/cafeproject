Ciao Caffe Windsor

Introduction

Ciao Caffe is located in the heart of East Windsor. It is a European style coffee shop with a great atmosphere and more importantly great coffee. Make Ciao your destination for breakfast, lunch or a late night snack. We offer various types of sandwiches, paninis, and desserts.
